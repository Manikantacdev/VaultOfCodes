Chocolate Cake

Prep Time: 45 mins | Serves: 8

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients.
  3. Add wet ingredients and stir.
  4. Pour into a pan and bake for 30 minutes.

Spaghetti Bolognese

Prep Time: 30 mins | Serves: 4

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 400g minced meat
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  1. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic.
  3. Add minced meat and cook.
  4. Pour in tomato sauce and simmer.
  5. Mix with spaghetti and serve.

Fluffy Pancakes

Prep Time: 20 mins | Serves: 4

  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Whisk in egg and milk until smooth.
  3. Pour batter onto hot griddle.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, flip and cook other side.

Caesar Salad

Prep Time: 15 mins | Serves: 2

  • 1 romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan
  • Caesar dressing
  • 1 grilled chicken breast (optional)
  1. Wash and chop lettuce.
  2. Add croutons and parmesan.
  3. Toss with Caesar dressing.
  4. Add grilled chicken if desired and serve.
